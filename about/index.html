About Me

Hi! I study cartography and GIS at the University of Wisconsin–Madison, where my M.S. research centers on interactive web mapping and data journalism. I’m passionate about bringing contemporary visualization tools, cartographic theory, and data together for ethical and effective visual storytelling. I previously received a B.A. in English and American Literature from Middlebury College.

In my free time, I enjoy self-powered exploration of the landscape on foot, skis, and bikes. You can find me on Twitter and Github, or reach me at nunderwood6@gmail.com.
