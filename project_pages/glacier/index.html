Portfolio About Resume
I investigated visitor movement patters in Glacier National Park by mapping the locations of Flickr photos taken within the park. Using an Observable notebook and the Flickr API, I acquired coordinates for about 30,000 photos taken within the park.

I used D3 to visualize the points with both hexbins and firefly styles in the browser, then pulled the maps into Illustrator with the New York Times' SVG Crowbar for final layout of the poster.

Tools: Observable, D3, SVG Crowbar, Adobe Illustrator
